Frequently Asked Questions

What is a crypto casino?

A crypto casino is an online casino that accepts cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.) for deposits and withdrawals. Most crypto casinos offer instant payouts, provably fair games, and don't require identity verification.

What does "provably fair" mean?

Provably fair systems use blockchain technology to prove that game outcomes are random and not manipulated by the casino. You can verify every bet result independently using cryptographic hashing.

Do I need KYC for crypto casinos?

Many crypto casinos don't require KYC (Know Your Customer) verification for deposits and withdrawals. However, some casinos may request KYC for large withdrawals or compliance reasons. We mark which casinos require KYC and which don't.

Are crypto casinos legal?

Crypto casino legality depends on your jurisdiction. Most crypto casinos operate under Curacao licenses and are legal in many countries. Always check your local laws before gambling online.

How fast are withdrawals?

Crypto casino withdrawals are typically instant to a few hours. Traditional online casinos can take 3-7 days. We mark withdrawal speeds for each casino so you know what to expect.

Can I use a VPN?

Some crypto casinos allow VPN usage, others don't. We clearly mark which casinos are VPN-friendly. Using a VPN where it's prohibited can result in account closure and loss of funds.
