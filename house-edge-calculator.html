Understanding House Edge & RTP

House Edge: The mathematical advantage the casino has over players. Lower is better for players.

RTP (Return to Player): The percentage of all wagered money that's paid back to players over time. Higher is better.

Formula: RTP + House Edge = 100%

Why This Matters

Over 1,000 bets of $10 each:

  • 1% house edge: You'd lose $100 on average
  • 5% house edge: You'd lose $500 on average
  • 10% house edge: You'd lose $1,000 on average

Playing games with lower house edge means your bankroll lasts longer and you have better chances of winning sessions.
