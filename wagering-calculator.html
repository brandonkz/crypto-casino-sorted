How Wagering Requirements Work

Wagering requirements (also called rollover or playthrough) determine how much you must bet before you can withdraw bonus funds. For example:

  • $100 bonus with 40x wagering = you must bet $4,000 total
  • $200 deposit + $200 bonus with 30x on both = ($200 + $200) √ó 30 = $12,000 total
  • Lower wagering requirements (20x or less) are better for players
  • Higher requirements (50x+) make it very difficult to withdraw
